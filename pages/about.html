  • Alok Ranjan Tiwari: Journey of a Young Media Entrepreneur

    Alok Ranjan Tiwari stands as a shining beacon of youthful ambition and unwavering determination in the world of media entrepreneurship. At a remarkably young age, he embarked on a journey that would not only defy norms but also redefine the way media influences society in India.

  • Early Passion for Words:

    From an early age, Alok displayed an extraordinary affinity for words and article writing. The written word had an inexplicable allure for him, and he found solace in penning down his thoughts and observations. Fuelled by his passion for writing, he decided to chart a path that revolved around the power of storytelling and impactful journalism.

  • A Bold Decision:

    Alok's decision to tread the path of media entrepreneurship at the tender age of 19 was a testament to his fearlessness and conviction in pursuing his dreams. Opting for a career in journalism and media rather than conventional professions, he was driven by the desire to make a positive impact on society through his words.

  • Founding Media Ventures:

    With a vision to transform the media landscape in India, Alok Ranjan Tiwari set out to create media ventures that would resonate with the changing times. Under his visionary leadership, he established Eternal Corporate Media Pvt. Ltd, an enterprise that would be a crucible for innovative and impactful media initiatives.

  • Editor-in-Chief and Media Maven:

    As the Editor-in-Chief of the renowned National Hindi News Magazine 'Abhyuday Vatsalyam,' and Business Magazine "Corporate Capital", Alok emerged as a media maven, steering the publication to new heights of influence and relevance. His editorial acumen and keen eye for impactful journalism captured the attention of readers across the nation.

  • Championing Change:

    Alok Ranjan Tiwari's journey as a young media entrepreneur has been marked by his constant endeavour to champion change and revolutionize the field of journalism. Embracing technology as a powerful tool, he has harnessed its potential to disseminate information and connect with audiences in new and innovative ways.

  • Empowering Voices:

    Through his media ventures, Alok has provided a platform for diverse voices and perspectives, empowering individuals and communities to be heard. His commitment to truth, fairness, and unbiased reporting has earned him the trust and respect of readers and industry peers alike.

  • Launching "Sashakt Bharat':

    A milestone in his journey, Alok Ranjan Tiwari spearheaded the launch of the coffee table book "Sashakt Bharat," a testament to India's growth and strength. Endorsed by eminent industrialists, the book showcased the nation's resilience and entrepreneurial spirit.

  • Inspiring the Next Generation:

    Alok's journey as one of the youngest media entrepreneurs in India serves as an inspiration to aspiring media professionals and entrepreneurs. He epitomizes the belief that age is no barrier to success, and with determination and passion, dreams can be transformed into reality.

Alok Ranjan Tiwari's indomitable spirit and dedication to the craft of media entrepreneurship have set him on an extraordinary path of making a lasting impact on the world of journalism As he continues to push boundaries and challenge conventions, he remains a beacon of hope and innovation, leading the way for a new era of media in India.
