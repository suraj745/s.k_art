  • Alok Ranjan Tiwari,

  • a dynamic corporate journalist and media entrepreneur, has carved a niche for himself in the world of journalism. His early decision to pursue this path at the age of 19 was a testament to his unwavering determination and passion for making a difference.


    Currently serving as the Editor-in-Chief of the prestigious National Hindi News Magazine 'Abhyuday Vatsalyam,' Alok is committed to delivering impactful content that resonates with readers nationwide. As the Executive Director of Eternal Corporate Media Pvt. Ltd, he blends innovation and creativity to create powerful media experiences that leave a lasting impression.


    Through his writings, Alok Ranjan Tiwari sheds light on the lives and accomplishments of prominent figures in the Indian industry. His words have the power to inspire admiration and motivation, making him a respected voice in the field of journalism.


    Beyond his professional pursuits, Alok is a passionate advocate for national unity and integrity. He firmly believes in the potential of technology to revolutionize journalism and information dissemination. With a vision to contribute to societal development, he actively participates in skill development initiatives aimed at alleviating poverty in India.
